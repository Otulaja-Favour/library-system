About Our Library

Welcome to our community hub of knowledge and learning. Discover our mission, history, and dedicated team.

Our Mission

We empower our community through free access to knowledge, fostering literacy, curiosity, and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

A vibrant, inclusive space where everyone can explore, learn, and connect through the power of information.

Our History

1900

Library founded with a small collection of 500 books.

1950

Expanded to include a children’s wing and public programs.

2000

Launched digital catalog and eBook services.

2025

Renovated main branch with modern tech and community spaces.
